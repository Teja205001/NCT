About Us




Mission:

Technologies at the conception to optimize queuing models related to OPDs, bed availability, and patients' admission processes in the hospital with an aim to provide better patient experience, outcomes, and efficiency in hospital operations and encourage collaboration and data-driven decisions.


Focus Areas:

‣ OPD level queuing models
‣ Bed availabilities
‣ Patients' admissions
‣ Medicines and consumable supply
‣ Inventory modules for management

Approach:

‣ Co-design and development with healthcare stakeholders
‣ User-centric design thinking and agile development methodologies
‣ Interoperability with existing systems and infrastructure
‣ Continuous testing, evaluation, and improvement

- Objective:

Develop a holistic hospital-centric solution, which would then extend to the city-wide module wherein technology-based processes could be used to reduce wait times, improve patient flow, and ensure resource utilization.
Building on work that already exists: Modules that the NIC has already developed are those which our solution will utilize and roll out in Delhi.

Team:
Health technology experts passionate about improving patient care and hospital operations in Delhi.
